# YouTube Video Card Structure

## Primary Content (Main Visual Level)

### 1. Hero Section
- **Video Title** (H1)
- **Channel Name** with link
- **Publication Date**
- **Video Duration**
- **Main Video Thumbnail** (large, clickable to open video)
- **Quick Stats**: Views, Likes, Comments count

### 2. Main Article Content
- **Introduction Paragraph**: Context-aware summary of what the video is about
- **Key Takeaways Section**: Bulleted list of main points
- **Main Article Body**: 
  - Well-formatted paragraphs with proper typography
  - Embedded screenshots at relevant moments
  - Pull quotes with timestamps
  - Subheadings for different topics covered

### 3. Interesting Moments Timeline
- **Visual Timeline Bar**: Horizontal scrollable timeline
- **Moment Cards**: Each showing:
  - Thumbnail screenshot
  - Timestamp (clickable)
  - Brief description
  - Quote if applicable

## Secondary Content (Collapsible/Tabs)

### 4. Transcription Panel
- **Full Transcript**: Formatted with timestamps
- **Language Selector**: Switch between original and translations
- **Search within transcript**
- **Download options** (TXT, PDF)

### 5. People & Entities Section
- **People Mentioned**: 
  - Photo/avatar
  - Full name
  - Brief biography (2-3 sentences)
  - Role in video
- **Terms & Definitions**:
  - Term name
  - Definition
  - Context in video

### 6. Translations Panel
- **Language Selector** for article translations
- **Side-by-side comparison** option
- **Translation quality indicator**

### 7. Video Metadata
- **Tags/Categories**
- **Related Videos**
- **Video Description** (original)
- **Links mentioned in video**

## Design Principles

### Visual Hierarchy
1. **Primary**: Article content, hero section, interesting moments
2. **Secondary**: Transcription, translations, metadata (tabs/collapsible)
3. **Tertiary**: Share buttons, related content, footer

### Typography
- Large, readable serif font for article body (blog-like)
- Clear heading hierarchy
- Monospace for timestamps and code/technical terms
- Comfortable line height and spacing

### Layout
- Single column for main content (max-width ~800px)
- Sidebar for secondary content (optional)
- Responsive design
- Clean, minimal aesthetic

### Color Scheme
- Light background, dark text (traditional blog)
- Subtle accent colors for links and highlights
- High contrast for readability
